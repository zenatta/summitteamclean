How Often Should You Clean Your House?

A good rule of thumb is to clean an occupied property at least once a week, but the truth is there is no ‘one size fits all’ rule regarding how often your space needs to be cleaned. We consider multiple factors when building a scope of work that suits all of your particular needs. Knowing the size of your property and its layout, as well as the different types of finishes in your space, the occupancy, and the frequency of use helps us make a recommendation on frequency and budget.
